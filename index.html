AI-powered pre-consultation assistant collecting structured clinical histories for faster, safer consultations.

Try Veritas!

The Challenge

Doctors spend up to 40% of consultation time collecting patient history, leaving limited time for diagnosis and treatment planning. This inefficiency affects both healthcare quality and patient satisfaction, creating a bottleneck in modern medical practice.

Our Solution

Veritas is an AI-powered clinical assistant that conducts comprehensive pre-consultation interviews, gathering patient and family medical history through natural conversation. By the time patients see their doctor, a complete, structured history is ready for review.



























Core Capabilities

AI

Intelligent Conversations

Natural language processing adapts to each patient's communication style, asking relevant follow-up questions based on symptoms described.

EHR

Structured Data

Automatically converts conversations into standardized, EHR-compatible formats that integrate seamlessly with existing hospital systems.

V/T

Multi-Modal Input

Supports both voice and text interactions, allowing patients to communicate in their preferred method for maximum accessibility.

S

Clinical Summaries

Generates concise, actionable summaries highlighting critical information for rapid physician review before consultation.

L

Privacy First

Built with healthcare compliance at its core, ensuring full HIPAA adherence and encrypted data handling throughout the process.

E

Empathetic Design

Emotion recognition technology adjusts conversation tone and pacing, creating a supportive environment for patients discussing sensitive health issues.

How It Works?

1

Patient Arrives

Before seeing the doctor, patients interact with Veritas via tablet, phone, or computer in the waiting room or from home.

2

Guided Interview

Veritas conducts a comprehensive interview covering current symptoms, medical history, medications, allergies, and family health background.

3

Smart Analysis

AI processes the conversation, identifying key patterns, red flags, and relevant historical factors that require physician attention.

4

Doctor Reviews

Physician receives a structured summary with highlighted priorities, allowing them to focus consultation time on diagnosis and treatment.
